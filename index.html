LIGO-Fermi Workshop

We are pleased to announce the second joint LIGO-Fermi workshop at Caltech in Pasadena, CA, March 14th & 15th.

The focus of the workshop will be on topics of joint interest to both collaborations, including science enabled from coincident GRB/GW events and electromagnetic follow-up. Our hope is to encourage collaboration in a hands-on, interactive way.

Location

The workshop will be held on the Caltech campus, in the Keck Laboratory, room 142. See the campus map for help finding the buidling.
