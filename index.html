LIGO-Fermi Workshop

We are pleased to announce the second joint LIGO-Fermi workshop at Caltech in Pasadena, CA, March 14th & 15th.

The focus of the workshop will be on topics of joint interest to both collaborations, including science enabled from coincident GRB/GW events and electromagnetic follow-up. Our hope is to encourage collaboration in a hands-on, interactive way. For this second meeting, we will include participation and presentations from Swift team members to allow a broader discussion of high-energy electromagnetic observations to GW candidates.
